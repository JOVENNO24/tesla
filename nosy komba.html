NOSY KOMBA

description:

Nosy Komba is a circular volcanic island, located in the northwest of Madagascar, 7km from Hell-Ville in Nosy Be.
The position of its geographical centre is 13°47 / 48°34.
The island covers an area of 25 km2. The island of lemurs measures 6.5km in its greatest length and 5km in its greatest width.
Its summit reaches more than 622m.
Nosy Komba is home to many lemurs of the same species, the macaco makis.
These small cuddly animals, which live in groups led by one or two females
populate the forests of the island and attract visitors who come specially to see them.
With no roads, cars or electricity, Nosy Komba is a peaceful place.
In this small corner of the world, one lives to the rhythm of the sun and the sailing pirogues, as if time had stopped.
This magnificent island, as much by its nature as by the gentle way of life of its inhabitants
charms the visitor as soon as he arrives, and bewitches those who linger there.
